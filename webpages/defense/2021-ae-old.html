Do

Please use no more than 150 words to list and explain the steps that you took to complete this artifact. Especially you may want to summarize what disciplinary or cross-disciplinary skills you have developed during the completion of the artifact.

I developed my own way of organizing ideas for long thesis-driven essays:

  1. Create a document that lists out the prompt, my first version of thesis, and my first touch on forming a thesis;
  2. Start by searching for the Wikipedia pages of the key words in prompt, and write down logic causations; start labelling the document using:
    • TODO Something that requires doing, but doing it now will break thinking flow;
    • OBS A topic that requires extra attention and further research;
    • DOC Great claim, but a document is required;
    • FORM Great claim, but need to be reformatted/put in other places;
    • DEL Included in the preparation documentation for completeness, consider deleting in final passage.
  3. Start resolving the labels in the previous step and add additional details; repeat step 2. and 3. until I am satisfied with my work;
  4. Start writing the essay.
Not only did I developed my essay-writing tactics, I also created more in-depth thoughts in system thinking (how change-over-time in plot and context effect each other; in-depth comparison, etc.); cultural and intercultural understanding and research between America and France; and also my skills in writing and expressing.
